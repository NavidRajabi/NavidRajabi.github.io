Navid Rajabi

 

PhD Student in CS at GMU

 

nrajabi 'at' gmu 'dot' edu

My name is Navid. I am a second year PhD student in Computer Science with the focus on AI at George Mason University. My research interest lies at the intersection of Computer Vision (CV) and Natural Language Processing, using Deep Learning techniques. This research area can be considered as a foundation for a wide spectrum of usecases, ranging from image captioning to video understanding/captioning, and Vision-and-Language Navigation (VLN).

 

Before joining George Mason University, I was a graduate research assistant at University of San Francisco and Illinois State University, where I received my master of science degree. Also, I was appointed as the Vice President of the university-wide Honor Society chapter at Illinois State University. During my master's degree, I was mainly working on the applications of Machine Learning and Deep Learning in Internet of Things (IoT) to enhance the performance and robustness of the Smart Buildings/Cities architectures. Prior to that, I received my bachelor of science degree in Computer Engineering (Software Engineering branch) from KNTU.
